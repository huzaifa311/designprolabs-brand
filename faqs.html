Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a website cost?

The cost of a website varies depending on factors like complexity, features, and design. A basic website can cost as little as a few hundred dollars, while a more complex website can cost several thousand dollars. Contact us for a personalized quote and learn how we can help bring your website vision to life.

How long will it take to get a new website?

The timeline for building a website varies depending on factors like complexity, features, and design. A simple website can take a few weeks to complete, while a more complex website can take several months. At Web-Ark, we work closely with our clients to establish realistic timelines and ensure that we meet project deadlines. Contact us to discuss your website needs and get started today.

Will my website be mobile-friendly?

Yes, we build all of our websites to be fully responsive and mobile-friendly, ensuring that your website looks great on all devices, including smartphones and tablets. With the majority of internet users browsing on mobile devices, a mobile-friendly website is essential for reaching your target audience and providing a positive user experience. Contact us to learn more about our mobile-friendly website design services.

What if I need help on my site down the road?

We offer ongoing website support and maintenance services to ensure that your website stays up-to-date and functioning smoothly. Whether you need help with updates, troubleshooting, or additional features, our team is here to provide the assistance you need.

Can I access the CMS myself?

Yes, we provide our clients with access to the backend of their website, allowing them to make updates and changes as needed. We also offer training and support to ensure that our clients feel comfortable navigating the backend and making updates to their website.

Will my Website Design be unique?

Yes absolutely. It will be designed exclusively for your organisation, product or service. We do not use “templates” or recycled designs.

What kind of websites do you build?

We've built portals, E-commerce, Informative, Blogs, Directories and much more, our experienced team is able to cater all your web design needs.

How much does a logo cost?

It all depends on how you want your logo to be. We offer different budget friendly packages starting from $42 which is the basic package mainly for the startups. Our best seller package starts from $85 and has some Smart design Solution Recommended For Small Scale Businesses. We also offer a complete package known as the ‘'The Ultimate' package for $170 which serves as a cutting-edge design solution and is recommended for all businesses.

Are there hidden, or additional fees for revising logos during my project?

No. All our packages have built-in revision cycle also, our designers are flexible enough to accommodate a few more revisions without any extra charges. We do not promise you to offer unlimited revisions as we do not believe in following an unrealistic approach.

How many logo samples do I receive?

It depends upon the package you will choose from the below mentioned packages:

  • Budget
  • Best Seller
  • The Ultimate

What is the procedure?

It all starts with our logo design briefing form which you can complete online or download as a Word file to email back to us. You will have an account manager and a lead designer who will discuss your project with you over the telephone. If you have asked us to look at other companies' brands we will research these on the web. We will then prepare an initial selection of prototype designs. These are intended to start a dialogue that will continue through a series of development stages to complete the design.

How do I keep check of progress?

Telephone and email have served us very well over the years and we have built great working relationships with hundreds of clients we have never met. However you keep a complete check on your order progress right from your Customer Panel (dashboard).

How do revisions work?

You will be given multiple design concepts by our designers. You will select a specific design for your logo and then we will make revisions according to your requirements. We will also ask you to give feedback on the designs already provided.

Do you design stationery?

Yes, stationery design can be provided at an additional fee, if its not included in your package selected. We give you artwork files to take it to your printer for letterheads, business cards, compliments slips and other specialized stationery you may require.
